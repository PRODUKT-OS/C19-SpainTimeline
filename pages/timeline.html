Has Covid-19 reached Twitter?

Spain has gone through many stages throughout the course of the pandemic, so many that we often try to remember what we had discussed with our family or friends last week without being able to do so. Many of us rely on the media, while others choose to use their favorite social networks, but what would happen if we could analyze all these stages through people's social interactions on Twitter? would they have any relation with the events presented by the media?


In this project, through Natural Language Processing, we try to answer all these questions!
